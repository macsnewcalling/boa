Important Notice

Aapka purana prank account kuch din baad permanently band kiya jayega. Kripya usmein se sare paise nikaal kar apne prank headquarter ke account mein daalein.

Is process ke baad, aapka new account activate kar diya jayega. 24 se 48 ghanto ke andar headquarter aapka verification karega.

Verification ke baad, aapka account aapke nearest zip code par transfer kar diya jayega. Uske baad aap apne nearby branch se withdrawal karke apna fund use kar sakte hai.
